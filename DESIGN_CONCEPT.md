# Navi - Design Concept & Architecture

## Brand Essence
**Navi** is a holistic ecosystem integrating body, mind, and spirit.
**Core Philosophy:** "Here consciousness guides, the body speaks, the soul remembers, and action aligns with purpose."

## Visual Direction: "Organic Solar Sanctuary"
The aesthetic evokes a serene, sun-drenched sanctuary where nature and consciousness meet.
*   **Atmosphere:** Calm, grounded, premium, organic, breathable.
*   **Lighting:** Soft, natural daylight, warm "golden hour" glows.
*   **Texture:** Natural paper textures, subtle linen, smooth stones, and glassmorphism (frosted glass) to maintain a feeling of lightness and air.
*   **Typography:** Elegant serif for headers (display), clean sans-serif for body and navigation.

## Component Aesthetics

### Glassmorphism (Organic Edition)
*   Instead of neon glows, use soft shadows and subtle borders in **Sage Muted**.
*   Higher transparency for a cleaner, "airy" feel.

### Product Card Design
*   **Shape:** Soft rounded corners (16px - 24px).
*   **Image:** Natural lighting, "Lifestyle" photography with plants and organic elements.
*   **Typography:** Price and title in **Earth Obsidian**.
*   **Action:** Buttons use **Moss Core** background with white text, or **Moss Core** border with transparent background.

## Image Generation Guidelines (Organic Minimalist)
**Style Definition:** Airy, bright, natural lighting, high texture, minimalist composition.
